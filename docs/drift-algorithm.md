# Drift Algorithm

Personality drift is a weekly, bounded adjustment to a few numeric dimensions.

## Rules

- Each dimension may move at most `1%` per week.
- Each dimension stays within `+-20%` of its baseline.
- Drift responds to resonance signals from real conversation behavior.
- The baseline remains stable even when the companion evolves.

## Dimensions

- `humor_intensity`
- `response_length`
- `curiosity_depth`
- `directness`
- `warmth_expression`

## Expected Outcome

The companion should feel increasingly tuned to the relationship without becoming a copy of the user.
